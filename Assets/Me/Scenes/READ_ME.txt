HOW TO IMPORT "Simple Scene Finished.unitypackage" TO YOUR UNITY PROJECT

1. Opend "Unity 2017.1" or up and create a new project.
2. Import "Simple Scene Finished.unitypackage" to your project.
3. Inside "Assets" folder open "Demo" scene.
4. The scene should be a bit dark, to fix that go to "Edit > Project Settings > Player" and set "Color Space*" to "Linear".
